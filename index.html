Low-carb effectiveness checklist

Carb consistency Keep net carbs near your target. For many ketogenic approaches this is often 20-50 g/day, but individual tolerance varies.
Protein adequacy Do not under-eat protein. It helps preserve lean mass during weight loss.
Electrolytes and fluids Low-carb dieting can increase water and sodium loss early on. Consider hydration, salt, magnesium and potassium from food where appropriate.
Trend over single reading Early drops are often water and glycogen. Judge progress by weekly trend, waist changes and adherence.

Deployment note

This version is a single-file app. Upload only this index.html file to the root of your GitHub repository. In Vercel, use a static/other project with no build command and no output directory. The app should open directly without React, Vite, Node, npm or a build step.
Clinical note: Ketosis, fasting and weight trend interpretations are educational estimates only. This app is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat or monitor disease.
Metabolic Track stores data locally on your device/browser. Keep your own backups before clearing browser data.
